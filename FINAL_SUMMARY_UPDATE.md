# Final Research Summary Update

This release adds an interpretive Research Summary to the Claim Passport.

- Opens from the Claim Passport toolbar or summary callout.
- Synthesizes the stored Claim Passport, Research Graph, and replay into one page.
- Shows the research question, recorded outcome, evidence/verification counts, failed routes, and recommended next step.
- Offers Plain Language, Technical, and Evidence & Assurance views.
- Works without an API key and does not create unsupported claims.
- Supports copying the summary for reports or submissions.

No backend research execution behavior was removed or replaced.
